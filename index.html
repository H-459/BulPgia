A random, 4 digit number is generated and the Guesser needs to guess it.

At each turn the Guesser enters a 4 digit number, and the result says how close it is to the answer by giving:

  • Bull - a black dot. This means that you got a correct number in the right position.
  • cow - a yellow dot. this means that you got correct number, but it's not in the right position.

The Guesser tries to guess the answer in the fewest number of turns.

The mystery number does not have the any repeating digits - 1441 is not a valid mystery number.
